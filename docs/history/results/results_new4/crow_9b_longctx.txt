## Root Cause

The flakiness stems from a **race condition in the async DB connection pool** triggered by the recent change from 5 to 10 connections. The increased pool size exposes timing-sensitive contention when multiple test workers attempt to acquire connections simultaneously. The test fails intermittently with timeout errors when the pool cannot provide a connection within the expected window during high-concurrency scenarios.
