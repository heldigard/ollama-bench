- Migrated billing service from Node 18 to Node 22 LTS with Stripe webhook rewritten in TypeScript at /src/webhooks/stripe.ts and deletion of billing.js.
- Updated signature verification environment variable from STRIPE_SECRET_KEY to STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET; added retry logic configured for max 5 retries with a base delay of 200ms using exponential backoff.
